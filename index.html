About

I am a fourth-year doctoral student in Cognitive Psychology at the University of Virginia.

My main work is on affect (moods and emotions) and value (kinds of good).

I am a developer intern at the Center for Open Science.

I am a fellow in the International Max Planck Research School on the Life Course.

Research

I am primarily concerned with affect, or value in the most general sense.

I study affect with questions like:

How does affective experience (mood) change value calculations (evaluations)?

How are positive and negative things expressed?

Are there different kinds of value that can be quantified and calculated?
